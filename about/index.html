Tegan Brace

Tegan Brace is a visual artist and sommelier living and working in Chicago since 2007. She received her BFA from Appalachian State University in 2007 and her MFA from the School of the Art Institute of Chicago in 2014.

Her work has been featured at Roots & Culture, Comfort Station, MCA Chicago, Demo Space, Acre, Adds Donna, and in Timeout Chicago and Didactic for Syntax Season.
